Onion juice for hair growth, is it really effective?

  • Matin Rostami
  • 2022-08-14
  • No Comments

Using onion juice for hair growth is a traditional and old method. Maybe this method seems strange at first. But it is entirely scientific and proven. Around 80 percent of men and 50 percent of women suffer from hair loss worldwide. As we grow older, androgens might cause hair loss. But according to researchers in Baghdad, there’s something you can do to stop those nasty androgens from stealing your hair: onion juice. Then, those looking for a remedy for unexplained hair loss may have used onion juice to grow their hair as an effective remedy. In the following, We’ll tell you how onion juice promotes hair growth and beauty.

Thanks to onion juice for Hair growth

It is already known that onions not only help with colds and digestive problems but also keep the vascular system young. However, onion has other properties that make it a real beauty weapon.

The scientists were able to prove that onion juice can accelerate hair growth. For their study, the researchers at the University Hospital in Baghdad treated the scalps of 38 subjects suffering from hair loss twice a day with onion juice. After just two weeks, the researchers noticed new hair sprouting from the bald spots. After four weeks, this was the case for 74 percent of the participants in the study. And after six weeks, 87 percent of the volunteers had their hair back.

Can onion juice help with severe hair loss?

If your hair is falling out in large quantities or if you have bald spots on your head, treatment with onion juice will not be successful. As rich as the ingredients in the onion are, they cannot sprout new follicles.

Can any type of hair loss be repaired with onion juice?

Not at all; onion juice does not affect hair loss due to genetic or disease-related causes or hair loss due to emotional stress. Onion juice home remedies can have a positive effect if your hair loss is due to nutrients or inflammation. To achieve the desired effect, you must know the cause of hair loss.

A doctor who examines the scalp and reads your general state of health in the blood count will help you to find the cause.

  • In general, you should know that the juice of an onion serves as a support. If you are under constant negative stress, the hair loss regulates itself with your focus on more peace in life.
  • You must consult your doctor if you lose hair due to illness or medication.
  • Diffuse hair loss without a diagnosed cause is, in many cases, diet-related.

Whether and to what extent you experience a visible effect is primarily based on the causes and reasons for the hair loss. Because onion juice is a natural product, you can try its use and not worry about any risks or side effects.

Even if the hair growth-promoting effect is absent, you will not damage the scalp or the follicles by using onion juice.

Facts about onion juice for hair growth

How onions juice stimulates hair growth? Scientists have not (yet) been able to find an explanation for this. However, various theories exist about how onion juice could cause hair to sprout. The sulfur from the onion is said to help supply the hair roots with the nutrients they need to grow. Since sulfur is also found in keratin, an important component of hair, it could be responsible for hair growth. Onions also have antibacterial and antioxidant properties. This protects and cares for the scalp, which is also important for hair growth, as hair can only thrive on a healthy scalp. Then onion juice for hair growth and scalp.

Also, onions contain a wide variety of nutrients, including vitamin C, various B-family vitamins, calcium, potassium, iron, phosphorus, manganese, and magnesium, as well as antioxidants, phenolic compounds, and anthocyanins. Onions have Flavonoids such as kaempferol and quercetin with an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effect. Then shampoo and oils can also be made from onion to improve dry and damaged hair and reduce itching and dandruff. Onions contain the enzyme catalase, known for its anti-aging effects, which means that onion juice can also minimize age-related hair loss. Treatments with onion juice can improve blood circulation in your scalp so that the hair follicles are again supplied with sufficient nutrients and oxygen. Now, we know, There are good reasons why the ancient Egyptians and healers in Asia swore by onions as a hair restorer. The onion is, therefore, a real miracle weapon for the hair and scalp.

Doesn’t your hair smell terribly like onions?

Not at all! We have often read that the hair stinks after the onion hair mask. But it’s just often done wrong! To avoid the smell, one should make the onion hair mask together with the lemon juice. The yogurt also neutralizes the smell very well. Mixed the onion juice with the lemon juice and yogurt, the mask almost no longer smelled onions unpleasantly.

Besides these, the smell of onion is unpleasant, and using these compounds may bother you with the smell of onions. But don’t worry if your hair smells very bad after using onion juice. You can get rid of the bad onion smell by doing several things. First, wash your hair thoroughly with shampoo after the onion cleanses and rinse it out several times; the scent will disappear by itself. By the time your hair is air-dried at the latest, nothing should remind you of your treatment with onion juice.

Also, you can just add a few drops of fragrance oil to the onion juice to cover the smell. Essential oils made from lavender, rosemary, or mint are particularly suitable for this.

How long should onion juice stay on the head?

After about 20 to 30 minutes, wash off the onion mask. Onion juice is rich in sulfur, and if it is not washed properly, onion sulfur remains in the skin and causes skin irritation, which is one of the disadvantages of onion juice for hair.

Possible side effects of using onion juice on the skin:

If you have sensitive skin, one of the side effects of onion juice on your hair may be irritation, redness, and itching of the scalp.

Maybe you have a question, how many times a week should onion juice be used for hair? It is better not to use it more than twice a week.

Onions juice for fuller eyebrows

Anyone not blessed with lush brow growth can also reach for the onion because exactly the sulfur compounds that work wonders on the head should also help with the eyebrows. You can either dab the juice of onion onto your eyebrows using a cotton swab or peel the onion, quarter it, cut off the edges and rub the piece of onion directly over your eyebrows. With both variants, the brows should be worked on for about five minutes, then let everything dry briefly and wash off again with a cleaning lotion.

How to get onion juice?

It is best to blend the onion in a high-performance blender (with one tablespoon of water to help if necessary) to a pulp-like, fine mass. The onion juice can be obtained easily by pressing the pulp through a fine sieve (e.g., tea strainer). Alternatively, you can take a clean kitchen towel and squeeze out the juice.

This is how it works: onion juice for hair growth.

1. Onion juice for hair growth

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Onion Juice
  • Cotton Pad

Method:

Using a cotton pad, saturate it with onion juice.

Dab the onion juice onto your scalp with the saturated cotton pad.

Massage your scalp for a couple of minutes once your entire scalp is covered.

After applying the juice, leave it on for 15 to 60 minutes.

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair.

Every two days, you can submit this application.

2. honey and onion juice for hair growth

Raw Honey, Onion Juice While it accelerates hair growth, it keeps hair moist and healthy.

Ingredients:

  • Mix 2 tablespoons of onion juice
  •  1/2 tablespoon of raw honey

Method:

Mix onion juice and raw honey until you get a smooth mixture.

Apply the mixture to the scalp and massage gently in circular motions.

After waiting half an hour, wash it with shampoo.

You can use it 2-3 times per week.

3. olive oil and onion juice for hair growth

Olive oil, when dealing with dandruff, and onion juice accelerate hair growth. It gives the hair a silky softness.

Ingredients:

  • Three tablespoons of olive oil
  • a tablespoon and a half of onion juice

Method:

Combine the olive oil and onion juice until well combined.

Apply the mixture to the scalp and massage gently in circular motions.

Leaving the oil on for two hours is recommended.

Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair.

Every two days, you can apply.

4. coconut oil and onion juice for hair growth

Coconut oil is an incredible antibacterial agent. Furthermore, it is a highly penetrating oil that boosts the scalp’s ability to absorb onion juice.

Ingredients:

  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  •  Two tablespoons of coconut oil
  • 5 drops tea tree oil Mix until smooth.

Method:

Mix onion juice, coconut oil, and tea tree oil until smooth.

Apply the mixture to the scalp and massage for a few minutes.

Wash it with shampoo after waiting half an hour.

You can apply every two days.

5. egg and onion juice for hair growth

Protein found in eggs helps nourish and maintain hair’s health, while onion juice stimulates the scalp to promote hair growth.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp Onion Juice
  • 1 Whole Egg
  • Shower Cap

Method:

Mix the onion juice and egg until smooth.

Massage this mixture into your scalp and down the length of your hair.

Wear a shower cap once your hair and scalp have been completely covered in the mixture.

Wait for about 20-30 minutes.

Wash your hair with cold water and shampoo.

Using a mild sulfate-free shampoo, wash your hair in cool water. (It prevents the egg from “cooking” and controls the smell when placed in cool water.)

You can apply 1-2 times a week.

6. castor oil and onion juice for hair growth

Castor oil contributes significantly to the growth of hair. According to animal studies, it thickens your hair and keeps it in excellent condition. Combining it with onion juice will minimize hair fall and increase hair volume.

Ingredients:

  • Two tablespoons of castor oil
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice

Method:

Combine the castor oil and onion juice well.

Apply the mixture to your scalp and massage it gently in circular motions.

Leave the oil on for about an hour.

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

You can apply every two days.

7. Potato and Onion Juice for hair growth

Potato juice is another ingredient that has growth-boosting properties. It contains many nutrients like vitamins B and C, iron, zinc, and niacin. It helps nourish your follicles and ensures healthy hair growth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp potato juice
  • 1 tbsp onion juice

Method:

Mix potato juice and onion juice until smooth.

Apply the mixture to the scalp

Massage your scalp in circular motions for 10 minutes.

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

Every two days, you can apply it.

8. ginger and onion juice for hair growth

Ginger is renowned as a natural anti-inflammatory and has been used for this purpose in both homeopathy and Chinese medicine. Aside from boosting blood circulation, it is also useful for minimizing hair loss and improving hair quality.

Ingredients:

  • One tablespoon of ginger juice
  • One tablespoon of onion juice

Method:

Mix ginger juice and onion juice.

Apply the mixture to the scalp and massage gently in circular motions.

Wash it with shampoo after waiting half an hour.

You can apply every two days.

9. garlic and onion juice for hair growth

Garlic is an abundant source of minerals such as calcium, sulfur, and zinc, all of which are essential for hair growth. Combined with onion juice, it improves hair growth rate and thickens hair simultaneously.

Ingredients:

  • One teaspoon of garlic juice
  • One tablespoon of onion juice

Method:

10. fenugreek and onions juice for hair growth

Fenugreek is rich in protein, vitamin C, and iron. This product reduces hair loss, fights dandruff, and adds vitality and shine to your hair. Besides providing sulfur and protein, onions also stimulate hair follicles, developing long, thick hair. Taking fenugreek and onion juice will help you grow your hair and prevent hair loss.

Ingredients:

  • A handful of fenugreek seeds
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice

Method:

Grind fenugreek seeds into a powder and add onion juice to make a paste.

Apply the paste to your hair and scalp.

Wash it off with shampoo after 15 minutes.

11. Fenugreek, coconut milk, and onion for hair growth

Coconut milk moisturizes your hair and eliminates dryness. Combining fenugreek, coconut milk, and onion enhances the strength and moisture of your hair.

Ingredients:

  • Three tablespoons of fenugreek powder
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  • One tablespoon of coconut milk

Method:

Mix fenugreek, coconut milk, and onion until they form a paste.

Apply this mask to your scalp and hair.

Leave it on for half an hour

Use mild shampoo and water to wash it off.

12. fenugreek, aloe vera, and onion juice for hair growth

The combination of fenugreek, onion juice, and aloe vera gel is excellent for soothing the hair. These ingredients’ anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties promote hair growth and soothe your scalp.

The ingredients:

  • Two tablespoons of fenugreek seeds
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  • Two tablespoons of aloe vera gel
  • One teaspoon of castor oil

Method:

Fenugreek seeds should be soaked in water overnight and ground into a paste.

Combine castor oil, aloe vera gel, onion juice, and fenugreek paste.

Apply this hair mask to your hair and scalp.

Leave it on for an hour.

Use a mild shampoo to wash it off.

13. Fenugreek, onion, and rice paste with water for hair growth

Fenugreek seeds provide a boost to the growth of hair due to the presence of amino acids that aid hair growth. Furthermore, it contains vitamins C, B, and E, which promote hair growth.

Rice water is rich in amino acids and vitamins, including vitamins C, B, and E. In addition to making your hair soft and thick, it can also help reduce frizz.

Facts:

  • Four tablespoons of fenugreek seeds
  • Half a cup of rice water
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  • One teaspoon of almond oil

Method:

In a cup of water, soak fenugreek seeds overnight.

Mix the ingredients after grinding them into a paste.

Put this hair mask on your scalp and hair.

Leave it on for 35-45 minutes

Use a mild shampoo to wash it off.

14. Fenugreek, Indian gooseberry, yogurt mask, and onion juice for hair growth

Indian gooseberries contain antioxidants and amino acids that help fight hair loss and strengthen hair.

Fenugreek seeds contain anti-inflammatory and antioxidant compounds that can help eliminate dandruff and soothe your scalp.

Protein in yogurt promotes hair growth and nourishes hair follicles. Dandruff and scalp infections can be prevented by the healthy bacteria in yogurt.

The ingredients:

  • One tablespoon of fenugreek seeds
  • Four tablespoons of yogurt.
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  • One tablespoon of Indian gooseberry powder
  • One teaspoon of curry leaf powder

Method:

Overnight, soak fenugreek seeds in water.

Grind the seeds into a paste and mix with yogurt, Indian gooseberry powder, and onion juice.

Apply this hair mask gently to your hair and scalp.

Leave it on for 45 minutes. 

Use a mild shampoo to remove it.

15. Fenugreek, Hibiscus, and Coconut Oil and Onion Juice for Hair Growth

Seeds of fenugreek inhibit fungus growth to help eliminate dandruff. To promote hair growth, coconut oil increases blood flow to the scalp and nourishes hair follicles.

The ingredients:

  • And two tablespoons of fenugreek seeds
  • 2-3 hibiscus leaves
  • Two tablespoons of onion juice
  • Four tablespoons of coconut/castor oil

Method:

Let the seeds soak in a cup of water overnight.

To make the paste, grind the soaked seeds and hibiscus leaves together.

Combine the onion juice and coconut oil with the dough.

Apply the mask to your scalp and hair.

Allow 15-20 minutes for the drying process

Then wash off with a mild shampoo.

16. Lemon, linseed oil, soy yogurt, and onion juice for hair growth

Ingredients:

  • One medium-sized onion (about three tablespoons of onion juice)
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Linseed oil
  • 2 tbsp organic soy yogurt without sugar

Method:

Combine all the ingredients into a liquid mass, but keep it creamy. 

Apply this to slightly damp hair

Wrap your hair in a thin cloth and then in a warm towel

Leave it on for about 30 minutes

Then wash it off thoroughly with a shampoo.
